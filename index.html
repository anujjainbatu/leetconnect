Track LeetCode Progress with Friends

LeetConnect is the ultimate Chrome extension for coding study groups. Track your friends' LeetCode progress in real-time, get notifications when they solve problems, and stay motivated together!

Powerful Features for Coding Teams

Real-time Stats Tracking

View total problems solved, difficulty breakdowns, and current rankings for all your friends in one place.

Smart Notifications

Get instant notifications when friends solve new problems. Stay motivated and celebrate achievements together!

Ranking Changes

Track ranking improvements and drops with visual indicators. See who's climbing the leaderboard!

Multiple Sorting Options

Sort friends by rating, questions solved, or alphabetically. Find the perfect view for your team.

Smart Caching

Instant loading with cached data while fetching fresh updates. Never wait for your stats again!

Auto-refresh & Settings

Customizable auto-refresh intervals (5-60 minutes) and notification preferences. Control your tracking experience.
